A work in progress.

I’m Jeremy Elder, a Minnesotan designer and since 1999 I’ve been creating responsible and honest work. I’m a fan of clarity over embellishment. I’m keen on taking a project from exploration to design, and bringing it to life with HTML, CSS and Javascript. While most of my work ends up on a screen, I also dig the stuff that ends up on walls and shelves. To that end I‘ve tackled identity, print, and packaging projects for everything from breweries to churches, and events to causes.

I get my inspiration from my wife, kids, many hours on a bike, my faith and loads of music. One day I hope to have self portrait that isn’t as cliché as a vintage black and white print of me staring off into the distance.

Currently, I’m a Senior Experience Designer for Life Time where I help maintain our enterprise design system, serve as an experience lead for several web properties, and also help drive our accessibility efforts.
