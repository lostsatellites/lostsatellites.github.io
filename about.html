Hi, Glad You‘re Here

I’m Jeremy Elder, a Minnesotan designer and since 1999 I‘ve been distilling concepts into responsible and honest work. I seek out clarity over embellishment through a variety of strategic and design channels in order to build appropriate and useful experiences.

Currently, I’m a Senior Experience Designer for Life Time where I help maintain our enterprise design system, serve as the experience lead for several web properties, and also help lead our accessibility efforts.

While most of my work ends up on a screen, I also dig the stuff that ends up on walls and shelves too so I tackle identity, print, and packaging projects for everything from breweries to churches, and events to causes.
