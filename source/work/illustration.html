Background

There aren’t many projects I work on where vectors are not part of the equation. In some cases it’s a full branding effort, for others it may be a lockup or simple graphics, and others yet it could simply be icons or UI elements.

You might notice that I’m not after a particular aesthetic, instead I prefer to seek out a style that serves each project the best.

I’ve skimmed the archives for a few favorites to share here and I‘ll leave it to Dribbble to show some more. Designs are primarily created with Adobe Illustrator and Affinity Designer.
